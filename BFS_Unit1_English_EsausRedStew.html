🍲 UNIT 1

Esau's Red Stew

A bowl of soup that changed everything

Genesis 25:29–34 (WEB)
🍲 Jacob's camp, long ago…
📖 Read the Story

One day, Jacob was cooking a big pot of lentil stew. The stew was red and hot, and it smelled wonderful.

His brother Esau came home very tired and hungry from the field. Esau said, "Please give me some of that red stew! I am so hungry!"

Jacob said, "I will give it to you — but first, sell me your birthright."

Esau said, "I am about to die of hunger! What good is a birthright to me?" So Esau ate the bread and the lentil stew. He got up and left. He didn't care about his birthright anymore.

🍽️ Today's Food Words
🫘
lentil A small, round seed used to make soup. Very filling!
🍲
stew A hot dish with vegetables and/or meat, cooked slowly.
🍞
bread Esau ate bread with his stew. A common food in the Bible.
🔥
hungry Feeling like you really need to eat. Esau was very hungry!
